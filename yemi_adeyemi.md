# Yemi Adeyemi

**Role:** Smart Contract Auditor  
**Location:** Ibadan, Nigeria  
**Bio:** Self-taught security researcher who hunts vulnerabilities in Base protocol contracts for bug bounties.

---

## Story: The Zero-Day

Yemi found it at 2am — a reentrancy bug buried inside a yield aggregator contract on Base. The protocol had $4.2 million in TVL. His hands were shaking. He had two choices: exploit it quietly and walk away rich, or report it and hope the team honored their bug bounty.

He made tea. He thought about the people whose USDC sat in that contract — freelancers, students, small savers. People like his younger sister, who had just put her first $300 into DeFi.

He wrote the disclosure report instead. It took four hours to document properly. He submitted it at 6am.

The protocol team responded in 90 minutes. They patched within 24 hours. The bounty came three days later: $18,000 USDC, straight to his Base wallet.

It was the most money he had ever received at once. But what he kept thinking about was the message from the protocol founder: *"You could have taken everything. Thank you for not."*

He pinned it to his wall.
