# Ravi Subramaniam

**Role:** On-Chain Game Designer  
**Location:** Chennai, India  
**Bio:** Former mobile game developer who left a studio job to build permissionless games on Base.

---

## Story: The Game He Couldn't Own

At his old job, Ravi designed a puzzle mechanic so elegant that the studio's lead designer called it "the best thing we've shipped in three years." Six months later, the game was delisted. Server costs. The mechanic disappeared with it.

He quit the next week.

On Base, he built a simple puzzle game where every level completion minted a non-transferable badge to the player's wallet. No servers. No company that could pull the plug. The game logic lived in a contract. As long as Ethereum existed, so did his work.

The game had 200 players in the first month. Not viral. Not life-changing. But a player in Indonesia messaged him: *"I completed level 40 two years ago on your old game. It's gone now. I'm glad this one can't be taken."*

Ravi read it three times. Then he opened his IDE and started designing level 11.
