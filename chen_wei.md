# Chen Wei

**Role:** Onchain Data Analyst  
**Location:** Chengdu, China (VPN)  
**Bio:** Former fintech data scientist now building public dashboards tracking Base ecosystem health.

---

## Story: The Dashboard Nobody Asked For

Chen Wei built the dashboard for himself. He wanted to understand Base's fee revenue, daily active addresses, and contract deployment trends — so he pulled the data, cleaned it, built the charts, and published it.

He expected nobody to care.

Within a week, three protocol teams were embedding his charts in their investor decks. A journalist cited his TVL graph in a CoinDesk article. A Base core developer messaged him asking if he wanted to contribute to their analytics working group.

He had built it at night, after his day job, using free RPC endpoints and a lot of patience with rate limits. He had never intended it to matter.

*"Public goods are weird,"* he told his friend over tea. *"You make something because you needed it. Then you realize everyone else needed it too and nobody else was going to make it."*

He kept updating the dashboard every morning before work. It was the first thing he checked. It had become, quietly, the thing he was most proud of.
